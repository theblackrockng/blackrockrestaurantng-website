# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Restaurant staff and administrators at BLACKROCK — the head chef, front-of-house manager, and owner (Mr. Shegun Ogunsanya or delegated staff). They access this console from a desktop or tablet, typically at the start of a shift or during service. The primary task on any given session is reviewing and actioning today's reservations and enquiries; secondary tasks are editing menu content.

## Product Purpose

Internal admin console for BLACKROCK Restaurant & Lounge (Ikeja, Lagos). Manages reservations, guest enquiries, and menu content. Success looks like staff being able to process a day's reservations and enquiries in under two minutes, with no friction and no confusion.

## Brand Personality

Premium, warm, authoritative. The same confidence as the restaurant itself — refined, Nigerian-timeless. Never cold or corporate. The console is a backstage tool but it should reflect the same pride the front-of-house does.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards (Notion, Linear, Monday.com aesthetic — light backgrounds, candy-colored tags, rounded cards)
- Sterile hospital-admin UIs
- Overly minimal "startup-dark" tools that look like developer toys

## Design Principles

1. **Backstage refinement** — This is a tool, not a product page, but it carries the BLACKROCK identity. Every surface should feel considered, not just functional.
2. **Speed over ceremony** — Staff are often mid-service. Interactions must be fast, unambiguous, and require minimal cognitive load.
3. **Trust the dark** — The charcoal-and-gold palette is the identity. Resist the instinct to lighten or neutralize; the darkness IS the brand.
4. **Hierarchy earns its weight** — Typography and spacing do the heavy lifting. Decorative elements are earned, not defaulted to.
5. **Errors are recoverable** — Error states must be clear and provide a path forward. Staff should never feel stuck.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Keyboard navigation required (staff may use the console without a mouse at a POS-style setup). Focus indicators must be clearly visible against the dark palette.
